Welcome to the new version of this website. The old Jekyll site has been
rebuilt with [Astro](https://astro.build/) and TypeScript, with three goals in
mind:

1. **Works** — a proper [publications](/publications/) page, kept up to date.
2. **Writing** — a [blog](/blog/) I intend to update more regularly, with an
   [RSS feed](/rss.xml) you can subscribe to.
3. **Applications** — a dedicated [gallery](/apps/) for the interactive tools
   and visualisations coming out of my research: text reuse explorers, network
   graphs, and digital corpora.

All the old URLs still work — they redirect to their new homes. If you find
something broken, [let me know](/about/).
