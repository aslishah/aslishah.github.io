An interactive application for exploring the **Mujmal al-Hikma**, the Persian
epitome of the *Rasāʾil Ikhwān al-Ṣafāʾ* (Epistles of the Brethren of Purity).

> The app is hosted on Render's free tier, so it may take up to a minute to
> wake up on first load. If the frame stays blank, open it in a new tab.
