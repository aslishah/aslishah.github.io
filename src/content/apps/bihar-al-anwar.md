**Exploring the Oceans of Lights**: navigating the *Biḥār al-Anwār* of
Muḥammad Bāqir al-Majlisī (d. 1111 AH), the largest Arabic-language compendium
of hadith and the largest premodern book — roughly 12 million words.

The app visualises text reuse between the *Biḥār al-Anwār* and the OpenITI
corpus, helping to trace al-Majlisī's sources across the Shiʿi textual
tradition.
