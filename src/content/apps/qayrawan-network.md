A work-in-progress network visualisation of scholars, teachers, and students
in Qayrawān and Fatimid Ifrīqiya, based on the prosopographical data gathered
for my doctoral thesis, *Traditions of Learning in Fāṭimid Ifrīqiya
(296–362/909–973)*.
