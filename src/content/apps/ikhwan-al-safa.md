This is a working network from my stylometry project on the *Rasāʾil Ikhwān
al-Ṣafāʾ* (Epistles of the Brethren of Purity), rendered with
[Retina](https://ouestware.gitlab.io/retina/). Nodes represent text segments;
edges reflect stylometric similarity. Use the panel on the left to colour and
filter the graph.
