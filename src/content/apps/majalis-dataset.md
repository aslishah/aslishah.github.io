A browsable dataset built from al-Qāḍī al-Nuʿmān's *Kitāb al-Majālis
wa-l-Musāyarāt*, recording the sessions (*majālis*) held by the Fatimid
Imam-Caliphs and the figures — authorities, interlocutors, and secondary
participants — who took part in them.

Each row is keyed by episode, section, and paragraph, with the page
reference and source alongside the participants and the context of the
exchange, making it possible to trace who spoke with whom, and where, across
the text.
